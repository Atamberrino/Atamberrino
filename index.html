Why This project is Important to Me

My baby sister, Ava, was born with a congenital heart defect. She was kept in the local NICU for nearly a month before she was released, and she had two surgeries before the age of 1. She's now five years old and is living life to the fullest. The Children's Miracle Network has done so much to better equip hospitals to help more children like my sister, and I want to help them continue to assist children by promoting them and suggesting that people donate to the organization.
